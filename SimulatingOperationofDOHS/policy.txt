Defense Officer Housing Society - Terms and Policies

Membership Eligibility: Open to serving and retired defense officers meeting eligibility criteria.

Rights and Responsibilities: Members have rights to participate in decision-making and must adhere to rules.

Property Usage: Solely residential use allowed; modifications require approval.

Maintenance and Upkeep: Members responsible for property and common area upkeep.

Dues and Assessments: Timely payment of dues and assessments required.

Conduct and Behavior: Members must uphold society's reputation and resolve disputes amicably.

Security and Safety: Adherence to security protocols and personal property security required.

Amendments and Modifications: Management committee may amend policies with notice to members.

Enforcement: Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action by the management committee.

Governing Law: Policies governed by local laws, with disputes subject to local jurisdiction.
